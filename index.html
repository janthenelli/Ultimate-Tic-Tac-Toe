Ultimate Tic Tac Toe

Rules:

  • The basic rules of tic tac toe still apply here, you have to win three of the smaller games in a row in order to win the full game.
  • The next players move is dependant on your move. The cell you go in in the smaller game is the cell your opponent will be forced to go in on the bigger game.
  • If a box is already won/draw and your opponent sends you to that cell for your next play then the entire board becomes available for your next move.
  • Think carefully before you make a move and have fun!
  • Enabled cells, cells that are currently available for the current turn.
  • Disabled cells, cells that are currently unavailable for the current turn.
  • Cells with this background color denote games won by X.
  • Cells with this background color denote games won by O.
  • Cells with this background color denote games drawn.
